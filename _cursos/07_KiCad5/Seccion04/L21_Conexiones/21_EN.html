Connections
  • In this lesson we are going to connect the components of the schematic.
  • The tool to connect components is called Place wire and can be activated with the W hot key or with the Place wire button
  • Click on the start pin to start a connection and another click on the end pin to finish.
  • And so we make all the connections.
  • Although it is not necessary in this scheme we can connect the lower connection to GND .
  • Finally we save the schematic.
