D2 footprint
  • This is the datasheet of the led with a 0805 package.
  • And here is the footprint recommended by the manufacturer.
  • Let's go to the footprint editor to see if we find a 0805 package SMD led in any library.
  • If we filter by 0805 several footprints appear. The one that interests us is the led LED_0805_2012Metric.
  • I am going to copy the LED_0805_2012Metric footprint from the LED_SMD library into our library.
  • We will keep the name.
  • And as a destination our library.
  • Let's see the dimensions of the footprint.
  • Here you can see the dimensions of the KiCad footprint (in pink) and the datasheet (in green). Kicad's are smaller, so they don't work for us.
  • We are going to modify the footprint dimensions. We start with pad 2.
  • We are going to put the dimensions that the datasheet tells us.
  • As the new pad is larger, we have to modify the outline of the footprint.
  • We are going to put a smaller grid to have more resolution.
  • As Grid 1 I will select 0.1mm
  • As you can see the grid right now is 2.54mm
  • To activate the Grid 1, press the Alt 1 key.
  • We are going to measure the distance from the pad to the edge.
  • The horizontal distance is 0.3mm
  • And the vertical distance is 0.3mm too.
  • We are going to move the edge so that the distance from the pad to the edge is 0.3mm.
  • The white line is the courtyard that KiCad detects the overlap of components.
  • I am going to change the Grid 1 to 0.05mm for higher resolution.
  • I have to lower the top edge.
  • Now I have to join the vertical and horizontal sections.
  • The same with the bottom horizontal edge.
  • Now we are going to copy the dimensions of Pad 2 to Pad 1.
  • Now we have to move the edges around the Pad 1.
  • As we have modified the original dimensions of the led we are going to change the name of the footprint. We are going to call it as the Part Number of the led.
  • Go to the properties of the footprint.
  • And change the Value and Footprint name fields.
  • We already have the footprint ready.
  • Save the changes.
  • We are going to assign the footprint to the symbol. Go to the symbol editor.
  • And edit its properties.
  • Click the assign footprint button.
  • And choose the newly created footprint.
  • Finally rearrange the symbol text and save the changes.
