J3 symbol
  • We will see in this lesson how to create the symbol for the J3 connector. J3 is the output connector in which we will have 5V.
  • This is the connector I have chosen at Mouser. The part number is 826646-2 .
  • It is single row, 2 positions and 2.54mm pitch
  • This is the true image of the component.
  • We are going to create a symbol for the two pins. We open the symbol editor.
  • We search for the word conn . I am going to choose the Conn_01x02 symbol.
  • I am going to copy it in our library with the name PinHeader_1x2 .
  • Save the library.
  • Now we are going to edit the properties of the symbol.
  • And I add the fields Value2Show , Mounted , MPN and Manufacturer .
  • Reorder the fields in the schematic of the symbol.
