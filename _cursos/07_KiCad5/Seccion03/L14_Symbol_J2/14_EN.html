J2 symbol
  • We will see in this lesson how to create the symbol for the J2 connector.
  • This is the connector I have chosen at Mouser. The part number is 1746978 .
  • It is of 2 positions and pitch 5.08mm
  • We are going to create a symbol for the connector. We open the symbol editor and search for screw .
  • We are going to copy it into our library with the name Conn2P_PCB .
  • We are going to edit its properties to add the Value2Show , Mounted , MPN and Manufacturer fields.
  • We order the texts in the symbol and save.
