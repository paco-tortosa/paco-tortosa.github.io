BOM
  • To get the list of materials (BOM) we go to the schematic editor.
  • In the Tools menu there is the option to generate the bill of materials (Generate Bill of Materials ...)
  • There are several plugins to obtain different BOM types.
  • We are going to generate the BOM with the plugin boom_csv_grouped_by_value
  • A xml file has been generated. We open it with excel.
  • A slightly strange BOM comes out. In the next lesson I am going to show you a C # program to get a BOM in csv format.
