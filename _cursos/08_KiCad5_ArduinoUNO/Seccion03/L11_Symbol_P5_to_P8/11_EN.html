Hole symbol
  • For the P5 to P8 components, we are going to copy the Conn_01x01 symbol from the Connector_Generic library in our bookstore.
Steps
  • Open the symbol editor.
  • We look for it with the filter and save it into our library.
  • Save the library.
  • Add the Value2Show, Mounted, MPN and Manufacturer fields.
  • A hole has no associated component, so the field Mounted will be NO and the fields MPN and Manufacturer will be empty.
  • Order the fields in the symbol (use the 10mils grid)
  • And save the library.
