D2 and D3
  • The D2 and D3 diodes are to protect the reset lines of the two microcontrollers from overvoltages.
  • This is the diode recommended in the Arduino reference design. The manufacturer is Bourns and its part number is CD1206-S01575.
  • It has an SMD package 1206
  • In the Device library I found the D symbol.
  • It is a polarized component, the cathode, on the left, should be number 1, the anode, on the right, should be pin number 2.
  • I have copied it in the JAVIERM_LIBS library and added the usual fields in my designs.
  • This is the datasheet with its dimensions
  • The D_1206_3216Metric footprint is in the Diode_SMD library.
  • Its dimensions are practically the same as those recommended by the datasheet, so it is ok.
  • So I have copied the footprint to our library.
