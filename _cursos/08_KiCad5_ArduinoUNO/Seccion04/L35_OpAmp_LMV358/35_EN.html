LMV358 OpAmp
  • The OpAmp LMV358 is used to disconnect the 5V from the USB in the presence of power at the Jack. The 5V will come from the NCP1117 regulator and not from the USB.
  • This is the OpAmp recommended in the Arduino reference design. The manufacturer is Texas Instruments and the part number is LMV358IDGKR
  • The package is SMD and we can power it with 5V.
  • In the same package there are 2 op amps (2 channels).
  • In the Amplifier_Operational library is the symbol for this operational.
  • This symbol has 3 parts (or units). Unit A is an amplifier.
  • In Unit B is the other amplifier.
  • In the Unit C are the power pins.
  • I have copied it to the JAVIERM_LIBS library and added the usual fields in my designs.
  • These are the dimensions in the datasheet
  • In the library Package_SO is the footprint VSSOP-8_3.0x3.0mm_P0.65mm for this operational.
  • So I have copied the footprint in our library.
