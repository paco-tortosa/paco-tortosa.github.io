47uF
  • The datasheet for the NCP1117 regulator recommends two 10uF capacitors, one at the input and one at the output.
  • The Arduino reference design recommends that the value be 47uF
  • This is the capacitor that I found at Mouser
  • In the Device library I found the CP symbol for polarized capacitor.
  • I have copied it in the JAVIERM_LIBS library with the name C_47uF and I have added the usual fields in my designs.
  • These are the dimensions in the datasheet
  • In the Capacitor_SMD library there is the CP_Elec_6.3x5.4 footprint for this capacitor.
  • So I have copied the footprint in our library.
