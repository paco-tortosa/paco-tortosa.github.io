MCU ATMEGA16U2
  • For USB communications with the computer we are going to use another Microchip/Atmel microcontroller, the ATMEGA16U2.
  • This is the Mouser website where you can see the ATMEGA16U2. Its full part number is ATMEGA16U2-AU
  • I have chosen the TQPF-32 package because I am going to solder it by hand.
  • As ATMEGA328P we are going to power it with 5V.
  • In the library MCU_Microchip_ATmega I found the symbol ATMEGA16U2-AU
  • I have copied it in the JAVIERM_LIBS library and added the usual fields in my designs.
  • In the footprint library Package_QFP is the footprint TQFP-32_7x7mm_P0.8mm
  • I have copied it to the JAVIERM_LIBF2 library
