LP2985 Regulator
  • The U2 regulator generates 3V3 from 5V
  • This is the recommended regulator in the Arduino reference design. The manufacturer is Texas Instruments and the part number is LP2985-33DBVR
  • It has an SOT-23-5 package and can drive up to 150mA
