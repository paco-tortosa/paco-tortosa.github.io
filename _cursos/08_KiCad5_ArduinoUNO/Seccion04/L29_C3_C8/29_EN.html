C3 and C8 Capacitors
  • The datasheet of the ATMEGA16U2 microcontroller tells us that we have to place a 1uF capacitor on the UCap pin
  • This 1uF capacitor is C8
  • Additionally, at the output of the 3.3V regulator there is also a 1uF capacitor, the C3.
  • This is the capacitor that I found at Mouser
  • It is 1uF, supports up to 10Vdc and the package is 0805.
  • We already had a similar capacitor in our 100nF library. We copy it, change the value to 1uF, the part number and manufacturer, but keep the footprint.
