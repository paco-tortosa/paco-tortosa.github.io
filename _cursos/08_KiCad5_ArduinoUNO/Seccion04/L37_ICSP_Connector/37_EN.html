ICSP Connectors
  • Each microcontroller has an associated ICSP connector to be able to be programmed.
  • This is the connector that I found in Mouser. The part number is 5-146254-3.
  • It has 6 positions in 2 rows with a pitch of 2.54mm
  • In the Connector_Generic library I have found the Conn_02x03_Odd_Even symbol.
  • I have copied it to the JAVIERM_LIBS library with the name Conn_02x03_ICSP
  • In the library Connector_PinHeader_2.54mm there is the footprint PinHeader_2x03_P2.54mm_Vertical that is perfect for our component.
  • So I have copied the footprint into our library.
