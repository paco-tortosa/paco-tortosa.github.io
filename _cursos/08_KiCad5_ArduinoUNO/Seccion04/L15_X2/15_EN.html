X2 Connector
  • The USB X2 connector is USB 2.0 type B.
  • This is the connector that I found at Mouser. The manufacturer is CUI Devices and its part number is UJ2-BH-1-TH.
  • In this section I am not going to show all the steps to copy the components of the KiCad libraries into mine since we have already seen that in section 3.
  • In the Connector library I found the USB_B symbol.
  • And I have copied it in the JAVIERM_LIBS library (I have also added the fields Value2Show, Mounted, MPN and Manufacturer)
  • This is the datasheet of the Mouser USB connector.
  • And these are its dimensions.
  • The Connector_USB footprint library contains the USB_B_OST_USB-B1HSxx_Horizontal symbol
  • I have copied it to the JAVIERM_LIBF2 library
  • The KiCad datasheet and footprint have exactly the same dimensions, so we don't have to make any changes to the JAVIERM_LIBF footprint
