Solution
  • Copy the workspace WS004 and rename it as WS004b.
  • Go to the schematic.
  • Double click on Pin_Verde.
  • Check the "HW connection" option.
  • Search for the "Not" component.
  • Drag it to the schematic.
  • Connect it with the signal coming from "Pin_SW2".
  • Thus you are given to "Pin_Verde" the inverted signal of "Pin_SW2".
  • Build the project.
  • And transfer the program.
