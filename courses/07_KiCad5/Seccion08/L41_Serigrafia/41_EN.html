Silkscreen
  • I'm going to order the silkscreen on the PCB.
  • As the copper layer at the top (F.Cu) is selected, the text C2 does not see, it is covered by the copper track.
  • I am going to select the silkscreen layer of the top F.SilkS to be able to see the texts.
  • First I'm going to move the H1 and H2 holes so that they are aligned with the J2 connector.
  • I select H1 with the mouse.
  • I add J2 to the selection with the mouse holding down the Shift key.
  • And the same with H2.
  • For the alignment reference to be J2 I put the mouse on J2 and with the right mouse button I select the tool to align to the left (Align to Left)
  • I move the logo to the right.
  • I move the fiducial FID1 so that it does not overlap with H2
  • I move the rectangle now.
  • We move the text H1 and H3 down.
  • I move the text J2.
  • We move the text C1
  • We move the text FID1
  • We add text to the J2 connector to identify the poles. One text will be 24V and the other Gnd.
  • We move the text FID2
  • We add text to the J3 connector to identify the poles. One text will be 5V and the other Gnd.
  • We are done, save the PCB.
