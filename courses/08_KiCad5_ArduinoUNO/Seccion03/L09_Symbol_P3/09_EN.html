P3 symbol
  • For the P3 component, we are going to copy the Conn_01x10 symbol from the Connector_Generic library into our library.
  • This is the connector that I found at Mouser. The part number is 5-535541-8.
  • It is 10 positions in one row and with a 2.54mm pitch.
Steps
  • Open the symbol editor.
  • We look for it with the filter and we save it into our library.
  • Edit the properties of the symbol.
  • Add the Value2Show, Mounted, MPN and Manufacturer fields.
  • Reorder the fields as usual and save the library.
