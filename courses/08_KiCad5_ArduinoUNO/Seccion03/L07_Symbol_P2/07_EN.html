P2 symbol
  • For the P2 component, we are going to copy the Conn_01x06 symbol from the Connector_Generic library into our library.
  • This is the connector that I found at Mouser. The part number is 5-535541-4.
  • It is 6 positions in one row and with pitch 2.54mm.
Steps
  • Open the symbol editor.
  • We look for it with the filter and we save it into our library.
  • Save the library.
  • Add the Value2Show, Mounted, MPN and Manufacturer fields.
  • Order the fields in the symbol (use the 10mils grid)
  • And save the library.
