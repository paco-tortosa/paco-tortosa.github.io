Components I
  • In this section we are going to see the components that comes with the template.
  • In this lesson we are going to see the 6 symbols that KiCad have added in the template.
  • Let's start with the P1 connector.
  • The symbol (in red) is called Conn_01x08 and is in the Connector_Generic library.
  • The footprint (in green) is called Socket_Strip_Arduino_1x08 and is in the Socket_Arduino_Uno library.
  • Now let's see the P3 connector.
  • The symbol (in red) is called Conn_01x10 and is in the Connector_Generic library.
  • The footprint (in green) is called Socket_Strip_Arduino_1x10 and is in the library Socket_Arduino_Uno.
  • Now let's see the P2 connector.
  • The symbol (in red) is called Conn_01x06 and is in the Connector_Generic library.
  • The footprint (in green) is called Socket_Strip_Arduino_1x06 and is in the Socket_Arduino_Uno library.
  • Now let's see the P4 connector.
  • The symbol (in red) is called Conn_01x08 and is in the Connector_Generic library.
  • The footprint (in green) is called Socket_Strip_Arduino_1x08 and is in the Socket_Arduino_Uno library.
  • Now let's see the holes.
  • The symbol (in red) is called Conn_01x01 and is in the Connector_Generic library.
  • The footprint (in green) is called Arduino_1pin and is in the Socket_Arduino_Uno library.
