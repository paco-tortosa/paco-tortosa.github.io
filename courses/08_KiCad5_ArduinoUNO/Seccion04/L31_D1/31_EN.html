D1 diode
  • The D1 diode protects against negative voltages the power that enters through the X1 connector.
  • This is the diode that I found at Mouser.
  • Supports up to 1A and has a voltage drop of 1.1V
  • We already had a similar diode in our library, of 150mA. We copy it, change the value to 1A, the part number, manufacturer and footprint.
  • This is the datasheet with its dimensions
  • The D_SMA footprint is in the Diode_SMD library.
  • The KiCad footprint is somewhat larger than the datasheet dimensions, so the footprint is Ok.
  • I have copied the footprint to our library.
