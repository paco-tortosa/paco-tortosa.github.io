16MHz Crystals
  • On the PCB there are 2 microcontrollers, each with its 16MHz crystal. In the original Arduino reference design the crystals are different. I am going to use the same model for both crystals.
  • This is the resonator that I have found at Mouser. The manufacturer is Murata and its part number CSTNE16M0V51Z000R0.
  • The frequency is 16MHz and it has integrated capacitors.
  • In the Device library I found the Resonator_Small symbol.
  • I have copied it to the JAVIERM_LIBS library with the name Resonator_16MHz
  • This is the datasheet.

  • The dimensions of the datasheet match those of the footprint Resonator_SMD_muRata_CSTxExxV-3Pin_3.0x1.1mm from the Crystal library, so I copy it into our library.
