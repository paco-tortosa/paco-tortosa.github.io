Z1,Z2 Zeners
  • The Z1 and Z2 zeners are connected between the USB data lines D + and D- and chassis to protect the PCB from electrostatic discharges.
  • This is the recommended TVS in the Arduino reference design. The manufacturer is Bourns and its part number is CG0603MLC-05E.
  • The working voltage of this zener is equal to or less than 5V. The signals of the lines D + and D- for USB1.1 and USB2.0 do not exceed 3.3V so this TVS is perfect.
  • In the library Device I found the symbol D_TVS_ALT
  • I have copied it to the JAVIERM_LIBS library and added the usual fields in my designs.
  • This is the datasheet with its dimensions
  • In the KiCad libraries I have not found a footprint for a TVS with package 0603, but the one for the capacitor works for us. Since as you can see the KiCad symbol is somewhat larger than what the datasheet recommends.
  • So I have copied the footprint to our library giving it the name TVS_0603
