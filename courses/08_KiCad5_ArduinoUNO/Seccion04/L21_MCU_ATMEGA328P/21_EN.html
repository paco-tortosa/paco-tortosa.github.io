MCU ATMEGA328P
  • The microcontroller popularly known as Arduino UNO is the ATMEGA328P from Microchip/Atmel.
  • This is the Mouser website where you can see the ATMEGA328P. Its full part number is ATMEGA328P-PU and the manufacturer Microchip
  • You can see that the package is PDIP-28 and the power supply is 1.8V to 5.5V. In this design, the supply voltage that we will supply to the MCU is 5V.
  • In the library MCU_Microchip_ATmega I found the symbol ATmega328P-PU
  • I have copied it to the JAVIERM_LIBS library and added the usual fields in my designs.
  • In the footprint library Package_DIP is the footprint DIP-28_W7.62mm
  • I have copied it to the JAVIERM_LIBF2 library.
