X1 Connector
  • The Jack X1 connector is used to connect to external power.
  • X1 is connected to the input of a linear regulator that generates 5V. The maximum voltage input is 20V.
  • This is the connector that I found at Mouser. The manufacturer is Gravitech and its part number is CON-SOCJ-2155.
  • The library Connector has the symbol Barrel_Jack_Switch
  • And I have copied it in the JAVIERM_LIBS library.
  • In the footprint library Connector_BarrelJack is the footprint BarrelJack_Horizontal
  • I have copied it to the JAVIERM_LIBF2 library
