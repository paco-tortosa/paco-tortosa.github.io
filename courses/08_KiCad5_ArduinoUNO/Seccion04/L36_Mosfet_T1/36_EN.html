T1 Mosfet
  • The T1 mosfet is used to connect the 5V of the USB with the 5v that powers the board in the absence of voltage on the Jack.
  • This is the mosfet recommended in the Arduino reference design. The manufacturer is ON Semiconductor and its part number is FDN340P.
  • It is a P channel and a SSOT-3 package.
  • In the library Transistor_FET I found the symbol AO3401A
  • I have copied it into the JAVIERM_LIBS library with the name FDN340P and I have added the usual usual fields to it.
  • In the datasheet the only information related to dimensions is that it is an SuperSOT-3 package.
  • In the library Package_TO_SOT_SMD I found the footprint SuperSOT-3
  • So I have copied the footprint to our library
