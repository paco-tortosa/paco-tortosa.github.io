Leds
  • The board has 3 yellow leds and one green in 0805 format.
  • This is the yellow led that I have chosen.
  • And this is the green one.
  • In the Device library I found the LED symbol.
  • It is a polarized component, the cathode, on the left, should be number 1, the anode, on the right, should be pin number 2.
  • I copy it into the JAVIERM_LIBS library with the name LED_0805_Yellow.
  • I copy it again to create the green one (LED_0805_Green).
  • These are the dimensions of the datasheet
  • The LED_0805_2012Metric footprint is in the LED_SMD library.
  • The dimensions are practically the same. We copy the footprint LED_0805_2012Metric in our library.
