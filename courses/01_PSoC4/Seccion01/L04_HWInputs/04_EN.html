HW Inputs
  • Lets activate the red LED when pressing SW2, but this time only with hardware, no software is necessary.
Steps
  • Copy the workspace WS002 and rename it as WS004.
  • Go to the schematic.
  • Double click on Pin_SW2.
  • Enable HW connection.
  • Do the same with Pin_Rojo.
  • Connect Pin_SW2 with Pin_Rojo.
  • Go to main.c
  • Remove or comment out the line 22.
  • Build the project.
  • And transfer the program.
