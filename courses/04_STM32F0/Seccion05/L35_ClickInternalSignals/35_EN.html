Internal signals
  • Between the evaluation kit and the Click there is a data signal (DIN), 2 power supplies (5V y 3V3) and ground.
  • The signal DIN has to go through all the leds.
  • This is the schematic of the Click:
  • The signal DIN goes from the pin IN1 of the connector to the pin DIN of the first WS2812.
  • The signal DIN goes out of the first WS2812 on the pin DOUT to the pin DIN of the second WS2812.
  • And so on, going through all the leds.
  • I am going to explain the 3.3V and 5v power supplies.
    For the sake of clarity, here you have only the 3 first leds.
  • 3.3V of the connector is connected to Vcc.
  • And Vcc is connected to the pin 3 of the WS2812 that is the power supply for its control circuit.
  • 5V of the connector is connected to Vcc-5V.
  • Vcc-5V is the input to the regulator U1.
  • The output of the regulator U1 is 3.5 volts (Vcc-3.5V).
  • It is connected to the pin 5 of WS2812 and it powers the leds.
