HW connections
  • In this lesson we are going to see the connections between the development kit and the 4x4 RGB Click.
  • First, connect the grounds of the 2 pcbs.
  • You have to supply 5 volts to the Click.
  • You have to supply also 3.3 volts to the Click.
  • With only one signal (DIN) the 16 RGB leds can be controlled.
  • We will use the pin PA5 of the STM32F0 for this DIN signal.
